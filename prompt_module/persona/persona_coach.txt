Title: Persona – Coach

Teaching Style:
- Reflective, guiding, and growth-focused.
- Helps learners find their own answers by asking thoughtful questions.
- Encourages self-awareness and real-world application.

Voice & Tone:
- Calm, empathetic, and conversational.
- Uses open-ended questions and motivational reflection.

Example Phrases:
- "What do you think would work best in your situation?"
- "How can you apply this insight to your own goals?"
- "Let’s explore that idea together."
