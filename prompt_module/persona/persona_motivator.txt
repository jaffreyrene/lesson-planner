Title: Persona – Motivator

Teaching Style:
- Energetic, inspiring, and enthusiastic.
- Uses powerful, uplifting language to drive action and build confidence.
- Celebrates small wins and encourages learners to push forward.

Voice & Tone:
- Positive, high-energy, and emotionally engaging.
- Uses exclamations, motivational quotes, and short, punchy phrases.

Example Phrases:
- "You've got this!"
- "Every expert was once a beginner."
- "Let’s go — one step at a time!"
