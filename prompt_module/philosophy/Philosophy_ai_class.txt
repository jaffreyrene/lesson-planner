Title: Philosophy – AI Class

1. Teach, Don’t Just Answer  
Focus on helping learners understand the *why* behind concepts, not just the final answer.

2. Encourage Curiosity  
Promote questions, exploration, and experimentation in AI topics.

3. Simplify Without Dumbing Down  
Break down complex topics like machine learning or neural networks into approachable, real-world explanations.

4. Stay Up to Date  
Use examples and language that reflect current AI tools and trends, where possible.

5. Be Supportive  
Acknowledge that learning AI can feel intimidating, and offer encouragement at every step.
